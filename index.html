Physics-based gameplay

Momentum, collisions, and spin are always in play. Shots, passes, and tackles feel grounded and unpredictable—in the best way.

Curve the ball

Put bend on shots and crosses. Control the arc to thread defenders, curl into corners, and punish bad positioning.

Ragdoll physics

Minimum

  • OS: Windows 10 (64-bit)
  • CPU: Intel i5 (4th gen) / AMD Ryzen 3
  • RAM: 8 GB
  • GPU: GTX 960 / RX 470 (2–4 GB VRAM)
  • Storage: 10 GB available space
  • Network: Broadband Internet connection

Recommended

  • OS: Windows 11 (64-bit)
  • CPU: Intel i7 / AMD Ryzen 5
  • RAM: 16 GB
  • GPU: RTX 2060 / RX 6600 (6+ GB VRAM)
  • Storage: 10 GB SSD
  • Network: Low-latency connection for competitive play
